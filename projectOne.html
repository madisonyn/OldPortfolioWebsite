Soiled | UI Design

AfterEffects can be a tool for prototyping and communicating motion that ultimately will be built in code. In this exercise, I created an animated logo that transitions to a website interface. Using my knowledge of motion design and kinetic typography, I produced a short video that demonstrates the flow of the website UI as if someone was browsing the site.

My design process began with developing a company and brand identity. “Soiled” is a fictitious company that serves as a digital commercial space to purchase plants and related accessories.

I designed a logo that incorporates the company name while depicting its reference to the company’s main commodity, plants. The website itself is designed to be simple, playful, and intuitive. Features to note are the cursor that changes to a flower symbol when hovering over a clickable item, the flower symbol theme appearing in the cursor and loading page, and the opacity changes when a certain item is clickable.
