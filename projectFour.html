Swiss Psych Fest Poster Redesign

In this piece, my objective was to redesign an event or festival poster that contained text to explore typography and visual hierarchy, using a grayscale and lines. My poster aims to create visual intrigue as the title text weaves vertically through the piece to guide and encourage the viewer to shift their attention to different sections. I played with lines that were fashioned into a grid which produces a bold visual identity and provides a visible organizational structure.
