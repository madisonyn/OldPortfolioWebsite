Wetbrush Redesign | Product Design

In this project, we were asked to examine a household item and conceptualize a redesign that improves on some of the itemâ€™s shortcomings. My redesign centered on improving the ergonomics of the Wetbrush, a hairbrush known for its detangling properties. In this project, I emphasized ergonomics, sustainability, and functionality. To address ergonomics, I adjusted the handle to a rounded shape with a soft grip to increase comfort when holding. I also decided to change the material from a plastic silicone to oak wood which is known for its longevity and ability to be recycled, unlike plastic. My redesign also added a component that allows the user to raise a slip on the head of the brush that brings loose hair and debris to the top of the bristles, making it easier for the user to clean their brush and remove hair.
