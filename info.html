Welcome!

My name is Madison Yn and I’m a 3rd year design student with an emphasis in graphics and sustainability in the built environment at UC Davis. Throughout my design studies, I’ve come to resonate with humanitarian and sustainable design, and I hope to incorporate these principles in my future career as a designer, whether that be in a more digital sphere like graphic design or a physical environment, such as packaging design. I find myself interested in a variety of design subdisciplines and am passionate about gaining experience in the fields of graphic, product, motion, and UI/UX design!

In my free time, I love spending hours in the climbing gym and bouldering with friends. You can also find me watching a Greta Gerwig film, cooking curry (of all varieties), and being out in nature (specifically forests although bodies of water are also quite beautiful.)
