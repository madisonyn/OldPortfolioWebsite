Wayne Thiebaud Poster

Posters serve a variety of purposes, from informational to aesthetically provocative. In this project, I designed an informational poster regarding an exhibition featuring Wayne Thiebaud’s art at SFMOMA. My poster incorporates soft, dreamy colors to encapsulate the themes presented in much of Thiebaud’s work. I chose to depict a self-portrait of Thiebaud to provide a face to the artist, in addition to one of his more notable portrait paintings. The images and text perform symbiotically to create a cohesive piece that prefaces the essence of Thiebaud’s work.
